About me

In the childhood i was usual kid who was not at all interested in music. I went to school and stuff and had pretty much as boring life as everyone else. I graduated school at some point and went to the college.

It could long for eternity, but eventually i found a Musical Instrument at the roof of my house. Few months passed as I was studying music all by myself and I bought myself a guitar. Since that day my musical self-education went even quicker. During my educational process I always spent much time on theory. This is how I started growing as one of the strongest musicians among my friends.

I realized that I can help people in learning music, gathered teachers and organized music courses.

Nowadays I am really competent musician, but, as you can see, my story began pretty obvious. That means anyone can start learning music today and lay the foundation of future yourself as a Musician.
